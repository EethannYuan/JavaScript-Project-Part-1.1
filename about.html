What you need to know about spa!

A spa is a location where mineral-rich spring water (and sometimes seawater) is used to give medicinal baths. Spa towns or spa resorts typically offer various health treatments, also known as balneotherapy. These practices have been popular worldwide.

  • Origins of the term
  • Treatments
  • Recent trends
  • Types of treatments

Origins of the term

The term is derived from the town of Spa, Belgium, known since Roman times. Some say it originated from the Latin word "spargere", meaning sprinkle or moisten.

Treatments

A 'body treatment', 'spa treatment', or 'cosmetic treatment' is a non-medical procedure to help the health of the body. Often performed at resorts, day spas, or beauty salons.

Recent trends

In the modern world, spa therapies are linked to beauty, indulgence, and health. The spa industry is growing rapidly and wellness is at its core.

Types of treatments

  • Day spa, a form of beauty salon
  • Destination spa, a resort for personal care treatments
  • Spa town, known for healing water properties
  • Foot spa
